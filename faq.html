FAQ

In our store you can purchase bank cards of any countries. You can find them in the "Credit Cards" section, which is located in the main navigation menu. You can also buy bank cards at a discount in the appropriate section.
In the future, we will expand the range of our store, and here you can buy everything you need for good and productive work.

Buying from our store is only available to activated users.

You can top up your balance by clicking on it in the top menu, or by clicking on the icon .
Further on the payment page, you need to select the currency that is available for replenishing the balance.
We remind you that we do not fix your balance in US dollars. Your balance is stored in bitcoins, and payment for all services will be deducted from you in bitcoins, in accordance with the exchange rate.

After transferring bitcoins, you need to wait for three network confirmations.
After three confirmations, the money will be credited to your account within 15 minutes.
When transferring, indicate a high commission so that your payment is confirmed quickly.
Remember, in no case do not transfer money to the same address twice! Our automatic replenishment system only sees the first transaction, and credits funds for it!

After purchasing the card, you have 10 hours to view the card, after 10 hours the card will automatically open and it will be impossible to return money for it.
Before opening a bank card, you have a button for checking the card through a checker. When you click this button, $1 will be frozen on your balance, and if the card is invalid, $1 will be returned to your balance for checking the card and the cost of the bank card. If the bank card turns out to be valid, you will be charged $1 for checking the card through a checker.
Do not use the checker all the time. It is not necessary.
We do not accept refund requests for bank cards, everything happens automatically.
If you suspect that the check is not working correctly - write to us, we will definitely check.

The store has a special card verification system after opening. You can always check the card before opening.
