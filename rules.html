Rules

1. By registering, you automatically agree to the rules of the store.
2. Changing the rules is possible without notifying users.
3. All funds on the balance are stored in BTC
4. The cost of the material is indicated in $ and is converted into btc at the exchange rate at the time of purchase.
5. Refund of the deposit is not possible.
6. If you find bugs or vulnerabilities, report them via tickets
6.1. If you intentionally use bugs or vulnerabilities to profit, your account will be permanently banned.
7. After cleaning the section of your purchases, the administration will not be able to return the purchased cards to you. Save cards to your devices.
7.1. Save cards from the shopping section. The administration will not be able to return the purchased cards to you in case of a technical failure.
8. The administration has the right to block the account, without explanation.
9. If you lose access to your account, the administration will not be able to restore your data and access will be lost forever.
10. We are not responsible for the availability of funds on purchased cards.
11. For databases loaded with the possibility of a refund, the cost of checking the card is $1. If the card turns out to be invalid, you will be refunded the funds for checking the card and the cost of the card itself.
12. Refunds are possible only for invalid cards, according to the answers of our checkers.
13. After purchasing the card, you have 12 hours to open and check the card, after the time has elapsed, you will not be able to receive a refund and the card will be automatically opened.
14. You have 5 minutes to check the card after opening, after the time has elapsed you will not be able to request a refund for your card.
14.1. When the checker answers that the card is invalid, the money is automatically returned to the balance.
14.2. There is a limit on checking the card after opening. You can find the limits in the FAQ.
14.3. If the card is invalid, you won't be able to view its data.
15. Communication with support is possible only through the ticket system.
16. CVR - an approximate percentage of valid cards in the database, it is formed based on the results of checking random 20 cards from the batch.
17. For sellers
17.1. If you want to become a bank card provider, create a ticket and we will contact you.
17.2. When loading the database, we automatically take 20 random bank cards from it and check them for validity. The percentage of valid is written in the name of the database.
17.3. Withdrawal is available on BTC wallet from 0.001 BTC. A withdrawal request is created through the ticket system.
17.4. Every 4 days we will automatically check the validity of your cc databases, and if the validity differs, the database will be sent for re-moderation.
